The Best Movies according to Paige Miyaki

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Howl's Moving Castle

A slow-paced and relaxing Studio Ghibli film that focuses on confidence and the small things in life.

The Phantom of the Opera

Based on the broadway, a chilling tale of the cycle of abuse, love in juxtoposition to creative energy, and the drama of the performing arts.

Pride and Prejudice

A victorian romance about feeling worthy, family, and how pride conflicts with attraction.
